Cinnamon Star "Zimsterne" Cookies

  • Source: My German Recipes
  • Yield: 40 cookies

Ingredients

  • For the dough:
    • 400g ground Almonds
    • 390g Sugar
    • 3 Egg Whites
    • 12g Ground Cinnamon
  • For the icing:
    • 2 Egg Whites
    • 250g Confectioners Sugar

Instructions

Making the Dough

  1. Put all dough ingredients into a large bowl.
  2. Mix the ingredients until they have come together into a dough.
  3. Shape dough into a thick disk, wrap in saran wrap, and put into the refrigerator.
  4. Let the dough rest for at least 6 hours or overnight.

Making the Icing

  1. Whisk egg whites until they are fluffy and shiny.
  2. Add a third of the confectioners sugar to the eggs and whisk to combine.
  3. Repeat until all sugar is incorporated.

Making the Cookies

  1. Take the dough out of the refrigerator and roll it out onto a board that will fit into your freezer.
  2. Spread the icing onto the rolled dough, making sure it is even.
  3. Set the remaining icing aside.
  4. Put the iced dough into the freezer for two hours.
  5. After two hours, remove the cookies from the freezer and cut the cookies:
    1. Use a medium-sized star cookie cutter.
    2. Dip the cookie cutter in a small bowl of water.
    3. Cut a star out of the frozen dough and place it onto the baking sheet.
    4. Remaining dough can be kneaded and rolled again. Then, spread the remaining icing on it and repeat the process.
  6. Let the cookies dry for two hours.

Baking

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Bake cookies for about 8 minutes, or until the cookies can be easily lifted from the baking sheet. Be careful not to let the frosting tan.
  3. Let the cookies cool completely, then store in an airtight container.
